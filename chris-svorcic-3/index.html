The SWAT course explained

Simple Wave Analysis and Trading (SWAT) is a good starting point for any person who is looking for an easy way to get started in the FX Trading market.

As defined by Chris Svorcik “it is the big picture”, it is the result of the experience accumulated throughout his career. Mr. Svorcik justifies each of the trades he takes following his system as the goal and structure of SWAT.

In a detailed way and without keeping anything in the drawer, Mr. Svorcik provides the students with a systematic and clear trading methodology.

The SWAT course does not only explain how to analyse and give you signals, it also teaches and guides you to the Forex Market, starting from basic trading and psychology analysis to introduce you to instruments and helpful tools as: indicators, explanations, trading systems updates and channels, blogs, etc.

The course is eminently practical, explaining in detail the how and why of each action applied in the system. It also offers traders a roadmap.

A key point to consider when taking the course is that you not only will get Trading tools, risk management, market psychology, and everything you need to get started in the market; it also provides you with an excellent support while you are trading and when you need advice.

About Chris Svorcik

Chris Svorcik, awarded as the best FXStreet analyst of 2018 in the category of Brokers and Banks, was born in the Netherlands in 1979. He holds a degree in Banking and Finance from New York University and a degree in International Economics and Politics from Prague University.

He started working as a freelance trader to quickly become a mentor and responsible for the Trading Room for Winners Edge Trading. Then, he joined Admirals Markets as a Senior Analyst, Educator and content creator.

Since 2014 he has been part of the EliteCurrentSea group as a partner, his responsibilities are market analysis and formation in trading.
